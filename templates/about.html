About Me

I am a 24 year old senior Aerospace Engineering student minoring in Physics (graduating spring 2016). Most recently, I conducted research on the magnetic field effect on the convective thermal properties of magnetic fluids at UCF, for which I was awarded an honorable mention after my presentation at the Florida chapter of American Vacuum Society Florida chapter symposium. Currently, I am working on developing a web-based version of an Appropriate Technology Toolkit (AT2) that will be used around the world to help in the development and implementation of sustainable technologies. I am passionate about sustainability, energy production, efficient and novel designs, bio-mimicry and more. I am driven by my motivation to help tomorrow be the world we dream of today.
